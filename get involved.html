Get Involved

The Boparan Charitable Trust relies on support from individuals, colleagues, and businesses across the UK to ensure we can raise the vital funds to improve young lives. Whether you have a free weekend to volunteer, want to make a donation however big or small, or feel like taking on an extreme challenge, there so many ways to get involved.

Fundraising

Can you help us fundraise? From a sponsored walk to a mountain hike there are a number of ways to get involved on your own or in teams.

e have had some incredible people do some incredible things such as, bungee jumping, abseiling, sky diving and wing walking. There are so many ways and places you can do fundraising. For inspiring ideas about how you could help, download our fundraising pack.

“Fundraising for the Boparan Trust has been a truly fantastic experience! The team helped me put together my fundraising page as well as creating a special personalised poster just for me to help collect donations. On the day of my challenge, they provided me with plenty of support when I needed it the most, and after all the money had been collected in, I had a chance to see how my donations made a difference. A memory I will treasure for a long time. I look forward to my next challenge!” Marie, Wing Walking.

Volunteering

We would not be able to deliver our successful events without the support of volunteers. If you have a few hours to spare or some skills to share, please get in touch and help us transform young lives. Click to volunteer. “Volunteering for The Boparan Charitable Trust was not only a valuable addition to my CV, it was also great fun! While volunteering, I helped develop their marketing collateral and provided support at their events; including the Golf Classic, Football 5-A-Side and their Charity Abseil. I even had a go myself!” Sophie, Derby University Student.

Corporate

We are eternally grateful for the amazing corporate and business support given to the Boparan Trust, not least at our Flagship fundraising night “The Bright Star Ball” we would like to acknowledge some of these amazing organisations here.

Partnerships

The Boparan Charitable Trust prides itself on the partnerships it has created with suppliers and other charitable organisations with the joint aim of improving young peoples’ lives. We can only deliver the best equipment and services possible with the support of others, and we thank our partnerships for their continued support. If you think you or your company could help us, please get in touch.
